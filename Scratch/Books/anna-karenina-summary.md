# Anna Karenina – Novel Summary

Set in late 19th-century Russia, Anna Karenina centers on the doomed romance between the aristocratic Anna and Count Vronsky. Torn between societal duty and passionate desire, Anna’s affair leads to scandal, estrangement, and psychological turmoil. Interwoven with her tragedy are portraits of rural life, moral searching, and Tolstoy’s meditation on faith, family, and the pursuit of happiness.
