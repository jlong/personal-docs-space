# Madame Bovary – Novel Summary

# Madame Bovary

Madame Bovary charts the life of Emma Bovary, a doctor’s wife stifled by the banality of provincial life and romantic idealism. Seeking escape from her husband Charles’s dull routines, Emma pursues passion and luxury, accruing debts through adulterous affairs and excessive spending. As her illusions crumble and creditors close in, Emma resorts to suicide, leaving Charles and their daughter bereft. Flaubert’s realist narrative critiques bourgeois disillusionment, exploring themes of desire, societal pressure, and the conflict between fantasy and reality.
