# Dracula – Novel Summary

Written in epistolary form, Dracula follows English solicitor Jonathan Harker’s journey to Transylvania to assist Count Dracula with a real estate acquisition in England. Harker becomes a prisoner in Dracula’s castle before escaping and returning home to warn his fiancée Mina Murray, now Mina Harker, of Dracula’s supernatural threat. As Dracula relocates to England to spread his vampiric curse, Mina and Harker team up with Dr. Abraham Van Helsing and a group of allies—Dr. John Seward, Quincey Morris, and Arthur Holmwood—to pursue and destroy the Count. Stoking themes of modernity versus ancient evil, Victorian anxieties, and the power of faith and science, Bram Stoker weaves a tense, gothic battle between good and malevolent forces, culminating in a climactic chase back to Transylvania.
