# Jurassic Park

It's a new document. Let's see if things get deleted if I type too fast. What's going on? Have you ever seen the movie Jurassic Park?

It's one of Michael Crichton's best books. Published in 1990, it explores the dangers of genetic engineering and the hubris of trying to control nature.

They made a movie based on it. Steven Spielberg directed the 1993 film adaptation, which became a massive box office hit and spawned an entire franchise.

## Key Themes

- The unpredictability of complex systems (chaos theory)

- Science without ethical boundaries

- The illusion of control over nature
