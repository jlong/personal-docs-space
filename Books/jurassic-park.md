# Jurassic Park

It's one of Michael Crichton's best books. Published in 1990, it explores the dangers of genetic engineering and the hubris of trying to control nature.

They made a movie based on it. Stephen Spielberg directed the 1993 film adaptation, which became a massive box office hit and spawned an entire franchise.

## Key Themes

- The unpredictability of complex systems (chaos theory)

- Science without ethical boundaries

- The illusion of control over nature
