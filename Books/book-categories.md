# Book Categories

## Classic Literature & Literary Fiction

### 19th Century Classics
- **Pride and Prejudice** - Jane Austen (1813) - Romance/Comedy of manners
- **Jane Eyre** - Charlotte Brontë (1847) - Gothic romance/bildungsroman
- **Wuthering Heights** - Emily Brontë (1848) - Dark romance
- **Great Expectations** - Charles Dickens (1861) - Bildungsroman/Social commentary
- **A Tale of Two Cities** - Charles Dickens (1859) - Historical fiction
- **Moby Dick** - Herman Melville (1851) - Adventure/Philosophical fiction
- **The Scarlet Letter** - Nathaniel Hawthorne (1850) - Moral allegory
- **Madame Bovary** - Gustave Flauert (1856) - Psychological novel
- **Les Misérables** - Victor Hugo (1862) - Historical epic/Social justice

### Russian Literature
- **War and Peace** - Leo Tolstoy (1869) - Historical epic
- **Anna Karenina** - Leo Tolstoy (1877) - Literary fiction/Tragedy
- **Crime and Punishment** - Fyodor Dostoevsky (1866) - Psychological thriller
- **The Brothers Karamazov** - Fyodor Dostoevsky (1880) - Philosophical novel

### Adventure & Action
- **The Count of Monte Cristo** - Alexandre Dumas (1844) - Adventure/Revenge
- **Don Quixote** - Miguel de Cervantes (1605) - Satirical adventure
- **The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn** - Mark Twain (1884) - Adventure/Picaresque

## Gothic & Horror

- **Frankenstein** - Mary Shelley (1818) - Gothic sci-fi/Horror
- **Dracula** - Bram Stoker (1897) - Gothic horror
- **The Strange Case of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde** - Robert Louis Stevenson (1886) - Psychological horror/Gothic

## Ancient Epics & Classics

- **The Iliad** - Homer (circa 8th century BC) - Ancient epic
- **The Odyssey** - Homer (circa 8th century BC) - Ancient epic

## Modern Classics (20th Century)

- **1984** - George Orwell (1949) - Dystopian/Political fiction
- **The Great Gatsby** - F. Scott Fitzgerald (1925) - Jazz Age classic/Literary fiction
- **The Picture of Dorian Gray** - Oscar Wilde (1890) - Philosophical Gothic
